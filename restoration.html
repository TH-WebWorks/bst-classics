RESTORATION

Three distinct approaches to classic car restoration, each tailored to your unique vision and driving goals.

Factory Correct

The Purist's Choice

For collectors who value authenticity above all else. We meticulously restore your classic to its original factory specifications, using period-correct parts, materials, and techniques. Every detail is researched and executed to match the vehicle as it left the assembly line, preserving its historical integrity and maximum collectible value.

  • Original factory specifications maintained
  • Period-correct parts and materials
  • Historical research and documentation
  • Maximum collectible value preservation

Restomod

The Popular Choice

The perfect balance of classic style and modern reliability. We maintain your vehicle's iconic appearance while upgrading key systems for improved performance, safety, and comfort. Modern fuel injection, upgraded suspension, air conditioning, and contemporary amenities make your classic a joy to drive in today's world.

  • Classic exterior with modern performance
  • Updated engine management systems
  • Modern comfort and safety features
  • Reliable daily driving capability

Pro Touring

The Performance Choice

Built for enthusiasts who demand maximum performance. We transform your classic into a modern performance machine with serious horsepower, advanced suspension systems, high-performance brakes, and track-ready handling. These builds combine vintage aesthetics with cutting-edge performance technology.

  • High-performance engine builds
  • Advanced suspension and handling
  • Track-ready braking systems
  • Modern performance technology

Paint & Body Restoration

Show-Quality Finish

From extensive rust repair to show-quality paint jobs, we restore your classic's body to absolute perfection. Our skilled technicians combine traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques, handling everything from metal fabrication and bodywork to final clear coat application.

  • Rust removal and metal repair
  • Body panel replacement and fabrication
  • Professional paint preparation
  • Color matching and custom finishes

Engine & Performance

Power & Reliability

Whether rebuilding numbers-matching engines to factory specifications or creating modern performance powerhouses, we bring decades of experience to every build. From vintage carburetors to modern fuel injection systems, we understand the intricacies of classic automotive engineering.

  • Complete engine rebuilds
  • Performance upgrades and modifications
  • Fuel system conversions
  • Transmission and drivetrain work

Custom Upholstery

Interior Excellence

Transform your interior with period-correct materials or modern comfort upgrades. Our trusted upholstery partners specialize in both authentic restorations using original patterns and materials, as well as custom contemporary designs that enhance comfort and style.

  • Original pattern recreation
  • Custom seat design and comfort upgrades
  • Dashboard and trim restoration
  • Carpet and headliner replacement

Restoration Rescues

Project Salvation

Previous restoration gone wrong? We specialize in saving projects that others have abandoned or botched. No project is too challenging for our experienced team to evaluate and rescue. We've seen it all and have the expertise to turn disasters into dream cars.

  • Comprehensive project assessment
  • Correction of previous work
  • Parts sourcing and replacement
  • Complete project turnaround
