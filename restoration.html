With any complete restoration, you will receive a certificate of authenticity, as well as a comprehensive documentation package containing all records of the work carried out on your vehicle.

BUILD COST & PRICING

Brandon, like most reputable builders, works on a time and materials basis versus fixed final price bids. We understand this can create uncertainty, so we provide detailed estimates and regular updates throughout the build process to help you understand the scope and cost of your project.

There are several key decisions that chart the vision of the build, and we'll work with you to understand these choices and their impact on the final cost. Every project is unique, and we tailor our approach to meet your specific goals and budget.
