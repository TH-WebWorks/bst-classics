JOIN THE BST CLASSICS TEAM

Build your career with Iowa's premier classic car restoration shop. We're looking for passionate, skilled craftsmen who share our commitment to excellence and quality.

WHY WORK AT BST CLASSICS?

Join a team that values craftsmanship, quality, and passion for classic automobiles.

Work on Dream Cars

Every day you'll work on beautiful classic cars and custom builds. From iconic muscle cars to vintage trucks, no two projects are the same.

Learn & Grow

Expand your skills working alongside experienced craftsmen on complex restoration projects. We invest in our team's professional development.

State-of-the-Art Facility

Work in our modern 14,000 sq ft facility with professional equipment, climate-controlled paint booth, and everything needed for quality work.

Great Team Culture

Join a tight-knit team of professionals who love what they do. We foster a collaborative environment where everyone's input matters.

Competitive Pay

We offer competitive wages based on experience and skills. Your expertise and dedication are valued and rewarded.

Work-Life Balance

Monday-Friday schedule, weekends off. We believe in maintaining a healthy work-life balance for our team.

CURRENT OPENINGS

We're actively looking for talented individuals to join our growing team.

Automotive Restoration Technician

Full-Time

We're seeking an experienced automotive technician with a passion for classic cars to join our restoration team.

Responsibilities:

  • Perform mechanical restoration work on classic vehicles
  • Engine, transmission, and drivetrain work
  • Electrical system diagnosis and repair
  • Suspension and brake system restoration
  • Collaborate with team on complete restorations

Requirements:

  • 3+ years automotive repair/restoration experience
  • Strong mechanical aptitude and problem-solving skills
  • Experience with classic/vintage vehicles preferred
  • Own tools (specialty tools provided)
  • Valid driver's license

Body & Paint Specialist

Full-Time

Join our team as a skilled body and paint specialist to create show-quality finishes on classic restorations.

Responsibilities:

  • Metal fabrication and bodywork on classic vehicles
  • Panel fitting and alignment
  • Surface preparation and primer application
  • Custom paint mixing and application
  • Clear coat application and wet sanding/buffing

Requirements:

  • 3+ years body and paint experience
  • Experience with show-quality finishes
  • Metal working and welding skills
  • Knowledge of classic car paint systems
  • Attention to detail and pride in craftsmanship

Automotive Apprentice

Full-Time

Entry-level position for motivated individuals looking to build a career in classic car restoration. Learn from experienced professionals.

Responsibilities:

  • Assist technicians with restoration projects
  • Parts cleaning and preparation
  • Shop maintenance and organization
  • Learn mechanical and restoration skills
  • Support team members as needed

Requirements:

  • Passion for classic cars and automotive work
  • Strong work ethic and willingness to learn
  • Basic mechanical knowledge preferred
  • Reliable and punctual
  • Valid driver's license
