MEET THE BUILDER

Brandon Tjaden, the founder of BST Classic Rod & Custom, has built a successful career around classic vehicle restoration and customization. Since founding the company in 2010, Brandon has been involved in the automotive aftermarket as an enthusiast, builder, and craftsman.

Brandon Tjaden Founder & Master Builder

BRANDON'S STORY

Brandon's automotive journey began early with a deep passion for classic cars and traditional craftsmanship. What started as a personal interest in restoring vehicles has grown into a business with a focus on quality craftsmanship and attention to detail that exceeds customer expectations.

His automotive journey is known for restoring, modifying, and customizing classic cars and trucks with an unwavering commitment to authenticity and quality. Brandon puts his heart and soul into every project when it comes to the restoration, maintenance, and customization of vintage vehicles.

Using traditional tools and years of expertise, he brings classic cars back to life and puts them back on the road in better-than-factory condition. Every project reflects his dedication to preserving automotive history while enhancing performance and reliability.

EXPERTISE & PHILOSOPHY

All restoration services performed on your classic vehicle are carried out by experienced craftsmen in accordance with Brandon's exacting requirements regarding quality and the genuine character of components. His philosophy centers on producing a better-than-factory-standard while maintaining the authentic character that makes each classic special.
