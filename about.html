MEET THE BUILDER

Brandon Tjaden, the founder of BST Classic Rod & Custom, has built a successful career around classic vehicle restoration and customization. Since founding the company in 2010, Brandon has been involved in the automotive aftermarket as an enthusiast, builder, and craftsman.

BRANDON'S STORY

Brandon's automotive journey began early with a deep passion for classic cars and traditional craftsmanship. What started as a personal interest has grown into BST Classic Rod & Custom, focused on quality craftsmanship and attention to detail that exceeds customer expectations.
