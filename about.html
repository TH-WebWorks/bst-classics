ABOUT BST CLASSICS

For over 14 years, BST Classic Rod & Custom has been the premier destination for classic car restoration and customization in Iowa. Our team of passionate craftsmen combines traditional techniques with modern innovation to bring automotive dreams to life.

OUR STORY

Founded in 2010 by Brandon Tjaden, BST Classic Rod & Custom began as a small shop with big dreams.

What started as a personal passion for classic cars has grown into one of Iowa's most respected restoration facilities, serving clients across the Midwest and beyond.

Our Philosophy

Every classic car has a story, and every restoration should honor that story while enhancing it for future generations.

We've restored everything from family heirlooms to rare muscle cars, each project receiving the same level of attention and craftsmanship.

Today, our 5,000-square-foot facility in Norwalk, Iowa, houses state-of-the-art equipment alongside traditional hand tools, allowing us to tackle everything from complete frame-off restorations to custom engine builds and modern performance upgrades.

OUR JOURNEY

From humble beginnings to becoming Iowa's premier classic car restoration facility.

2010

The Beginning

Brandon Tjaden founded BST Classic Rod & Custom with a vision to create the Midwest's premier restoration facility.

Started in a modest garage with just basic tools and unlimited passion for classic automobiles.

2013

First Major Expansion

Moved to our current Norwalk location and expanded to a 3,000 sq ft facility.

Added our first professional paint booth and hired our first specialist team members.

2017

Award Recognition

Our work began receiving regional recognition with multiple awards at car shows throughout the Midwest.

Established reputation for show-quality restorations.

2020

Facility Upgrade

Expanded to our current 5,000 sq ft facility with state-of-the-art equipment.

Added dedicated engine bay and climate-controlled storage for client vehicles.

2024

Today

Over 90 completed restorations, 25+ awards, and a growing team of specialists.

Continuing to set the standard for classic car restoration in the Midwest.
