About Us

BST Classic Rod & Custom was founded by Brandon Tjaden in 2010. Brandon puts his heart and soul into every project when it comes to the restoration, maintenance, and customization of vintage vehicles. Using traditional tools and years of expertise he brings classic cars back to life and puts them back on the road.
