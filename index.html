We Don't Just Restore Cars, We Bring Dreams Back to Life

Your classic car isn't just a vehicle, it's a piece of history, a family heirloom, or the car you've always dreamed of owning. At BST Classics, we understand that emotional connection. That's why we don't just meet factory specifications, we exceed them, creating vehicles that are better than when they first rolled off the assembly line.

Factory-Plus Quality That Lasts

We don't just restore to original specs – we improve upon them. Every component is carefully selected, every detail meticulously crafted. The result? A vehicle that performs better, looks better, and lasts longer than the original.

Complete Peace of Mind

From the moment you contact us to the day you drive away, we handle everything. No subcontractors, no delays, no surprises. Just honest communication, transparent pricing, and results that exceed expectations.

Documentation That Adds Value

Every restoration comes with comprehensive documentation, including before/after photos, detailed work logs, and a certificate of authenticity. This documentation not only preserves your car's story but also increases its value for future generations.
