OUR 8-STEP PROCESS

Each restoration project follows our proven 8-step methodology, ensuring consistent quality and attention to detail throughout every build.

1

Initial Consultation & Assessment

We begin with a thorough evaluation of your vehicle and detailed discussion of your goals, timeline, and budget. This sets the foundation for your entire project.

2

Project Planning & Design

Detailed planning phase where we create a comprehensive restoration plan, source parts, and establish timelines. Every detail is documented and approved before work begins.

3

Complete Disassembly

Careful disassembly with each component catalogued, photographed, and assessed. We document everything to ensure proper reassembly and identify all necessary work.

4

Body & Frame Restoration

Frame and body work including rust repair, metal fabrication, and structural restoration. All work is performed to exceed original factory specifications.

5

Paint & Bodywork

Professional paint preparation and application using modern techniques and materials to achieve a finish that surpasses original factory quality.

6

Mechanical Restoration

Engine, transmission, suspension, and brake system restoration or upgrading. Every component is rebuilt or replaced to ensure reliability and performance.

7

Interior & Trim

Complete interior restoration including upholstery, dashboard, trim pieces, and all interior components using period-correct materials and techniques.

8

Final Assembly & Testing

Careful reassembly, system testing, and final adjustments. Comprehensive quality check and road testing to ensure everything meets our exacting standards.
