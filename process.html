THE 25-STEP BUILD PROCESS

How BST Classics Brings Iconic Machines Back to Life

  • Discovery & Design
  • Fabrication
  • Body Shop
  • Paint Shop
  • F.I.T Garage
  • Performance Shop

Phase 1: Discovery & Design

1

Initial Consultation & Assessment

Comprehensive evaluation of your vehicle and detailed discussion of your vision, goals, timeline, and budget to establish project foundation.

2

Project Scope & Planning

Detailed project planning phase where we create a comprehensive restoration plan, establish timelines, and document every detail before work begins.

3

Parts Research & Sourcing

Extensive research and sourcing of authentic parts, reproduction components, and modern upgrades to meet your restoration specifications.

Phase 2: Fabrication

4

Complete Disassembly

Careful disassembly with each component catalogued, photographed, and assessed to ensure proper reassembly and identify all necessary work.

5

Frame Inspection & Repair

Thorough frame inspection and any necessary repairs including straightening, reinforcement, and structural modifications for improved performance.

6

Custom Fabrication Work

Custom metal fabrication including brackets, panels, and modifications to accommodate modern upgrades while maintaining authentic appearance.

7

Suspension & Chassis Prep

Preparation and modification of suspension components and chassis for improved handling, safety, and performance characteristics.

Phase 3: Body Shop

8

Rust Removal & Repair

Complete rust removal and repair using advanced techniques including media blasting, cut-and-weld repairs, and protective treatments.

9

Panel Alignment & Fitment

Precise panel alignment and fitment to achieve factory-perfect gaps and smooth body lines that exceed original specifications.

10

Bodywork & Smoothing

Expert bodywork including dent removal, surface smoothing, and preparation for primer application using traditional and modern techniques.

Phase 4: Paint Shop

11

Paint Preparation & Priming

Meticulous surface preparation and primer application in our controlled paint environment for optimal paint adhesion and finish quality.

12

Color Matching & Application

Professional color matching and paint application using high-quality automotive paints and modern spray techniques for superior finish.

13

Clear Coat & Finishing

Clear coat application and finishing process including color sanding and polishing to achieve a deep, lustrous finish that exceeds show quality.

Phase 5: F.I.T Garage

14

Interior Restoration

Complete interior restoration including seat upholstery, dashboard restoration, and trim pieces using period-correct materials and techniques.

15

Electrical System Integration

Modern electrical system integration including wiring harnesses, lighting upgrades, and electronic component installation for reliability.

16

Glass & Trim Installation

Careful installation of glass, weatherstripping, and exterior trim pieces ensuring proper fit and weather sealing for long-term durability.

17

Hardware & Accessories

Installation of all hardware, accessories, and detail components including door handles, mirrors, and decorative trim elements.

Phase 6: Performance Shop

18

Engine Installation & Setup

Professional engine installation and setup including timing, fuel system connection, and initial break-in procedures for optimal performance.

19

Transmission & Drivetrain

Transmission installation, drivetrain alignment, and differential setup to ensure smooth power delivery and long-term reliability.

20

Brake & Suspension Systems

Complete brake and suspension system installation with modern upgrades for improved safety, handling, and stopping performance.

21

Performance Tuning

Engine tuning, carburetor or fuel injection setup, and performance optimization to achieve the perfect balance of power and drivability.

Phase 7: Quality Control

22

Systems Testing & Validation

Comprehensive testing of all systems including electrical, mechanical, and safety components to ensure everything operates to specification.

23

Road Testing & Adjustments

Extensive road testing and fine-tuning adjustments to ensure optimal performance, handling, and reliability under real-world conditions.

Phase 8: Delivery

24

Final Detailing & Presentation

Meticulous final detailing and presentation preparation to showcase your completed restoration in show-ready condition.

25

Owner Orientation & Delivery

Complete owner orientation covering all systems, maintenance requirements, and warranty information before your dream car goes home.
