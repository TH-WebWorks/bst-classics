1969 Pontiac Firebird

Complete Frame-Off Restoration 18 Months Completed 2023
Client: John & Sarah M. West Des Moines, IA
BEFORE
‚Üí
AFTER

The Challenge

This 1969 Firebird came to us as a true barn find - sitting for over 30 years with significant rust damage, a seized engine, and interior that had seen better days. The owners wanted a complete frame-off restoration to concours condition while maintaining numbers-matching components.

Our Solution

We completely disassembled the vehicle, media blasted the body, and rebuilt every system from the ground up. The original 350 V8 was completely rebuilt with period-correct specifications, while the body received extensive metalwork and a show-quality paint job in the original Carousel Red.

The Result

A stunning restoration that exceeded the customer's expectations and won Best in Show at the 2023 Iowa Classic Car Show. The attention to detail and quality craftsmanship speaks for itself.

Project Specifications

Engine: 350 V8 Complete Rebuild
Transmission: 4-Speed Manual Restored
Paint: Carousel Red Base/Clear
Interior: Black Vinyl Complete Restoration
Suspension: All New Components
Awards: Best in Show 2023
